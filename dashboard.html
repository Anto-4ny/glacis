Quantum Income — Empowering Lives Globally

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
Quantum Income is a global platform committed to transforming lives through digital giving and earning opportunities. By blending charity with technology, we enable people from every corner of the world to donate, support one another, and earn income remotely by becoming validators and referrers. Whether you're donating to help a cause or earning by validating transactions, you're making an impact.
Our mission is to create a decentralized ecosystem where giving meets opportunity. We aim to reward users who contribute their time and trust by verifying donation records, while also funding initiatives that support education, health, and financial independence globally. Quantum Income exists to build bridges between donors, validators, and dreamers in need.
To be the world’s leading donation and income platform where generosity is rewarded and global impact is measurable. We envision a future where millions thrive — not just through receiving donations, but through validating, referring, and building a self-sustaining digital economy powered by people, for people.

Starting Remote job as a Validator

Register as a validator by donating a minimum of 12 usd to start earning with remote jobs .

Validator Responsibilities

Validators verify user payments and earn 1 usd per validation. At least one validator must approve a payment.

Refer and Earn Program

  • Direct referrals earn 15% per validated account.
  • Level 2 referrals earn 5% per activation.

Payment System

Donations are done manually through Binance Wallet (BEP20): 821693759.

Transaction Proof Submission

  • Transaction Hash (TXID)
  • Payment method used (It should always be Binance)
  • Sender and receiver wallet addresses
  • Takes up to 24 hrs for your withdrawal to be approved

Dashboard Features

  • Account activation & verification status
  • Unique referral link
  • Membership panel for pending verifications
  • Wallet withdrawals (min: $1, 24hr approval)
